# Speedrun Splits
Record your PBs in all checkpoints in each platformer levels

- This mod adds a speedrun timer and checkpoint split tracker for platformer levels in Geometry Dash.
- It records your personal best time at every checkpoint and also stores a final completion PB for the level.
- During a run, the on-screen list shows your live split times and compares them against your saved bests.
- You can open Split Records from the pause menu to review, select, and delete stored checkpoint or final PBs.
- The interface is configurable, so you can choose where the timer appears and how the split list is displayed.
- It is designed to make platformer practice, route testing, and full speedrun attempts much easier to track.
